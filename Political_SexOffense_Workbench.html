Reference Guide

5-Level Verification Status Scale

Verified
Primary source confirmed: SOS filing / Ballotpedia w/citation / FEC record / voter registration. No contradictions.
Corroborated
2+ independent secondary sources (AP + local daily). No contradictions. Primary source not located or not public.
Presumed
Single secondary source. Party stated in news. No contradictions found. Primary source not available.
Unverified
No independent source confirms party. Daily Kos / advocacy site only. Or party not stated in any source.
Disputed
Sources conflict on party. Or serious factual accuracy concern. Flagged for review. Do not use in analysis.

⚠ Verification status applies to party affiliation only — offense details still require separate source confirmation.

Party Affiliation Verification Hierarchy

  • VERIFIED — SOS candidate filing / Ballotpedia with cited primary source / FEC record / voter registration
  • CORROBORATED — 2+ independent news sources (AP + local paper independently; not reprinting same wire)
  • PRESUMED — Single news source explicitly states party affiliation; no contradictions found
  • UNVERIFIED — Only source is advocacy site (Daily Kos, GOP Predators, etc.); party not independently confirmed
  • DISPUTED — Sources conflict, or primary source found that contradicts stated party

Inclusion Criteria

  • Held elected office (federal, state, or local)
  • OR ran as an official candidate (SOS filing)
  • OR held an official party position (appointed by party committee)
  • OR was a paid party staffer or campaign committee employee
  • AND: was charged with, convicted of, or pled guilty to a sex offense or CSAM
  • OR: made credible public self-admission — flagged as "No charge filed"
  • OR: resigned / was removed specifically in connection with a sex offense allegation

Not included by default: pastors, teachers, volunteers, donors, or voters — unless they also hold an qualifying position. The Daily Kos list includes these; we apply a stricter standard.

Key Source URLs

  • justice.gov/opa — DOJ press releases (gold standard for federal convictions)
  • ballotpedia.org — party affiliation, office history, misconduct tracking
  • fec.gov/data — federal candidate/donor records
  • followthemoney.org — state campaign finance, bipartisan, all 50 states
  • nsopw.gov — national sex offender registry
  • pacer.uscourts.gov — federal court records ($0.10/page)
  • Daily Kos — CajsaLilliehook — Republican predators series (research lead list; not a verification source)

NIBRS Offense Codes

  • 11A Rape — penetration without consent; body part or object; oral by sex organ
  • 11B Sodomy — oral/anal penetration without consent (legacy; many states now code as 11A)
  • 11C SA w/Object — instrument/object to penetrate genital/anal opening
  • 11D Fondling — touching private parts for sexual gratification without consent
  • 36A Statutory Rape — non-forcible sex with person under age of consent
  • 36B Incest — non-forcible sex between family members
  • CSAM — federal charge (18 U.S.C. §§2251–2260); combine with NIBRS code if also charged
